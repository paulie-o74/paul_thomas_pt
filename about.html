Paul Thomas O'Riordan

My message


After 20+ years of being active (but not neccessarily always healthy) I feel like I have developed a deep understanding of the key barriers to an active healthy lifestyle as well as some of the major motivators to help people make a change.
My goal is to help people realise their potential, and to do so I think it's important that people understand that we all start from different places.
All it takes is making a decision and reaching out for help to get started.

My Training philosophy


My idea of training is "We were built to do this, we were designed to move and there are only positive effects from being active". Unfortunately, the rhythm, distraction, obligations, and stress of modern life present lots of obstacles for people being active.
My speciality is helping people see the worth and importance in being active and helping them to realise their potential.
